WHAT IS WARHAMMER 40K?

Warhammer is a collection of games and lore owned and sold by Games Workshop Gourp PLC. Among these games is Warhammer 40000, a table-top turn-based strategy and war game unparallelled by other games of it's time. It's complex combat round combned with it's specialized armys of units and epic heros creates the strategic masterpiece of tabe-top gaming that it claims to be. With somany ways to enjoy the hobby incuding learning it's lore, painting it's figures, and collecting armies, it is easily enjoyable to those who can afford the time and cost.
